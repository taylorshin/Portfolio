About Me

I am a software engineer, AI researcher, and aspiring tech entrepreneur.

During my senior year at the University of California San Diego, I was part of a research group that published a research paper DeepJ that involved a deep neural network that composes polyphonic music.

In 2017, I joined the Web & Mobile team at Blizzard Entertainment and helped launch and maintain the Overwatch League website.

Currently, I am a graduate student at the University of California Irvine pursuing a M.S. in Computer Science with a specialization in AI and Machine Learning.

Portfolio

Projects

DeepJ is a deep neural network that generates original, classical music. It is trained on many different classical composers ranging from Bach to Tchaikovsky. The web application uses TensorFlow.js to generate samples of music based on pieces from the Baroque, Classical, Romantic, and Modern periods.

Publications
